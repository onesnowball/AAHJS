Survival Guide

Things to know
before you arrive.

⚡
Sign Early

Ann Arbor's leasing cycle is ruthless. Good apartments for the following fall go on the market as early as November–January. If you haven't started looking, reach out to these properties now.

🚌
The UM Blue Bus

It's 100% free with your student ID. Routes CN and NC connect North and Central Campus frequently. The Bursley-Baits route serves housing closest to North Campus. Download the DoubleMaps app for live tracking.

🛏️
Go Shared

A shared 2-bedroom is the most reliable way to stay under $1,200 in Ann Arbor's competitive market. Most of these properties have roommate-matching programs if you're arriving solo.

❄️
Gas Bills in Winter

Michigan winters are real. If gas is not included in rent, budget an extra $80–$150/month from November through March. The Courtyards includes gas — a meaningful saving.

🏫
North Campus Layout

ME, EECS, Aerospace, Nuclear, and Architecture are all on North Campus. The Duderstadt Center is your primary library and study space — open 24/7 during finals. Pierpont Commons has restaurants and a lounge.

🤝
Find Your Cohort First

Join the UMich ME grad GroupMe or Discord before signing a lease. Connecting with cohort members first can help you find a roommate naturally — and learn which neighborhoods most ME students actually live in.
